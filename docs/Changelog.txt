Bugfixes
- The RNG now seeds properly.
- If the clock has run dry, berries will still grow and time will still progress simply by saving and resetting.

Mechanical Changes
- Pressing B during a wild battle snaps the cursor to RUN.
- The game now asks if you want to use another repel after using one up.
- TMs are reusable.
- Buying more than 10 of any Poke Ball rewards you with a Premier Ball for every 10 you buy.
- Money penalty on loss uses the modern formula, which doesn't doesn't brutalize your wallet.
- Fishing always turns up an encounter, and is less frustrating.
- Text now scrolls 4x as fast.
- Most Marts now change their stock according to your badge count.
- The bag's items pocket can now hold 99 items instead of 30, with a storeable quantity of up to 999 of each.
- Healing items and vitamins have been moved to their own pocket.
- The Mach and Acro bikes have been merged together.
- Several normal moves can now be used as substitutes for HMs.
- If a Pokemon can learn Strength or Dive by HM, then they can use it in the field even without knowing the attack.
- HMs can now be forgotten normally.

Map Changes
- Trees stay Cut permanently.
- Two new post-game areas have been added featuring some Pokemon exclusive to FireRed or LeafGreen.
- After catching or defeating Deoxys, 4 meteors will appear around the region that will change its forme.
- Encounter rate on ocean routes has been halved, while underwater encounter rates have been doubled.
- Cut and Rock Smash now skip the prompt after their first use, similar to Strength.
- Mirage Island now has a (1*party size)/256 chance to appear.

Pokemon Changes
- Chinchou and Lanturn now have a 5% chance of holding a Deep Sea Scale.
- Carvanha and Sharpedo now have a 5% chance of holding a Deep Sea Tooth.
- All trade evolutions can be evolved using a key item obtained during the story. Trade evolutions involving an item need to be holding that item.
- Poison is survivable in the field.
- Items can now be moved between party members.
- Medicine can be used multiple times without returning to the bag.
- Hidden Power now displays its true type and power both in summary and battle.
- The reward for completing the Hoenn dex has been altered.
- Stat EVS now cap at 252 instead of 255.
- Feebas is now found at a 20% chance with a Good or Super Rod on non-Feebas tiles.

Wild Encounters
- Lunatone can now be found at night in Meteor Falls, replacing Solrock.
- Day is now considered 6AM - 6PM, while night is 6PM - 6AM.
- Roselia is now found on Route 117 at a 4% chance.
- Zangoose is now found on Route 114, replacing Seviper. Seviper can be found via Rock Smash here at a 10% chance.
- On Routes 102, 111, 114, 117, and 120, Surskit is now available at a 5% chance while surfing, and Goldeen's encounter rate has been boosted to 5%.
- Meditite is now available on the exterior of Mt. Pyre at a 10% chance, replacing Wingull.
- In Victory Road B1F, Meditite and Medicham are available at 10% and 5% respectively.
- In Victory Road B2F, Medicham is available at 15%.
- The other 2 Hoenn starters can be obtained by speaking to May after beating the game, and Wally after his first rematch.
- The 3 Johto starters can be found in Artisan Cave (Chikorita), Desert Underpass (Cyndaquil), and Steven's cave in Meteor Falls (Totodile) respectively at a 2% chance.
- Pokemon not normally available in Emerald have their met game changed to a legal game when caught or hatched, meaning they can be passed through Bank.

Misc Changes
- NPCs now have differently colored text depending on gender. Certain NPCs have their own text colors.
- The Ganlon, Petaya, Apicot, Salac, and Enigma berries are now available in various post-game areas.
