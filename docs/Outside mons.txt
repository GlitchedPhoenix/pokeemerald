Difficulty level mods
Normal = Nothing
Easy = Level - (1*badges) - Difficulty-adjusted trainers
       Level * 0.9 - Standard trainers
Challenge = Level + (1*(badges/2))
Brutal = Level + (1 * badges), difficulty-adjusted trainers over badge 7 use their next set
Unfair = Level + floor(1.5 * badges), difficulty-adjusted trainers over badge 7 use their next set, and after becoming Champion, use their next set after that
